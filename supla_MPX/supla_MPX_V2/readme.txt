depth sensor for liquids.
with differential pressure sensor.

with Mpx5010 maximum 1 meter deep.
with Mpx5050 maximum 5 meters deep.
with Mpx5100 maximum 10 meters deep.
selectable in WiFi config

Gpio 0 (D3) = wificonfig hold 5 seconds
Gpio 2 (D4) = status Led
Gpio 14 (D5) = MPX zero Calibration

Mpx compensation in Wificonfig from 500 to 1000 to compensate for different densities of liquids (normal for water around 750. a lower value display a higher value in the App, a higher value reduces the value display in the App)
